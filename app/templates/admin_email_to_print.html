How to Use Email-to-Print
For Users:
  1. Send an email to your-username@print.company.com
  2. Attach the documents you want to print
  3. Use subject line keywords for special options:
    • [URGENT] - High priority printing
    • [COLOR] - Force color printing
    • [SINGLE] - Single-sided printing
    • [COPIES:5] - Print 5 copies
  4. You'll receive a confirmation email with job details
